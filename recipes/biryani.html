Karachi Biryani Recipe

Behold! An authentic Karachi Chicken Biryani recipe with simple, easy-to-follow instructions (no curveballs!) and mouthwatering, traditional Pakistani and Indian flavor. This recipe includes tips on how to get fluffy rice, tender chicken, and the distinct biryani taste. Tested to perfection!

Biryani is an intricate rice dish made with layers of curried meat and rice. Given its use of adornments and luxurious finishes, itâ€™s no surprise that biryani has roots in Persian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • 1 skinless chicken cut into 10 or 16 pieces
  • 4 rice cooker cups of basmati rice. washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 tbsp of toasted and ground cumin and coriander
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 box of shan Bombay Masala
  • 2 tbsp of garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp of ginger paste
  • 3 tbsp of whipped room temperature yogurt
  • 3 chopped roma tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Place a large cooking pot (should be big enough for layering) over the stove over medium high heat.
  2. Pour in the oil and when hot add in the sliced onion till they are golden brown. remove with a slotted spoon on a paper towel.
  3. In the same oil over medium high heat, add in the butter, the chopped green chilies and the ginger garlic pastes.
  4. Next up, add in the boxed masala, the salt, cumin coriander powders, the chopped tomatoes and the yogurt. Reduce the heat to medium and cook till the oil separates.
  5. Once it is done cooking, remove this gravy in to a dish.
